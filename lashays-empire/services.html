Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my style last?

It depends on the style and how you care for it at home. Protective styles like braids and twists can last 2-8 weeks. Roller sets and silk presses typically last 1-2 weeks with proper care. I'll give you specific maintenance tips for your style.

Do you offer house calls?

Yes! I serve the Little Rock area and can come to you. House call fees may apply depending on location. Contact me to discuss your needs.

What are your hours?

I'm available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 3 PM. Weekend appointments may be available upon request.

Is consultation free?

Absolutely! I'm happy to discuss your hair goals and provide a personalized quote at no charge.
